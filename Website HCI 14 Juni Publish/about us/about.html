ABOUT US

Welcome to VKellogg's

At VKellogg’s, we take pride in being a multinational company renowned for our production of delicious and nutritious breakfast foods and snacks. Founded by Will Keith Kellogg in 1906, our journey began in Battle Creek, Michigan, United States, where our headquarters still resides today. With over a century of experience in food product innovation, VKellogg’s has become a household name and a trusted choice for families around the world.

Our History

VKellogg’s story began with a visionary named Will Keith Kellogg, who had a passion for creating wholesome and convenient breakfast options. From the creation of the iconic Corn Flakes to our wide range of cereals, snacks, and convenience foods, VKellogg’s has consistently set the standard for quality and innovation in the food industry. Our commitment to excellence has made us one of the leading cereal manufacturers worldwide.

Our Mission

At VKellogg’s, our mission is simple: to nourish families so they can flourish and thrive. We believe that a good day starts with a great breakfast, and we are dedicated to providing high-quality, nutritious, and delicious products that bring joy to breakfast tables everywhere. We strive to make every meal a delightful experience, offering a variety of options to suit different tastes and dietary needs.

We invite you to explore our wide range of products and discover the VKellogg’s difference. Whether you’re enjoying a bowl of our classic cereals, indulging in a tasty snack, or trying out a new recipe from our website, you are part of a tradition that spans generations and brings people together.


Thank you for choosing VKellogg’s. Here’s to many more delicious moments together!
